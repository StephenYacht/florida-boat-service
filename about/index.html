Where It Began

From Annapolis
to South Florida

Stephen Nemeth's career in marine electronics didn't begin in a classroom — it began on the docks of Annapolis, Maryland, where the standards for seamanship and marine craftsmanship are among the highest in the country. Annapolis — the sailing capital of the United States and home to the United States Naval Academy — is a city where boats aren't just recreational, they're a way of life. The boaters there are experienced, exacting, and unforgiving of poor workmanship.

Working on those docks and aboard those vessels, Stephen developed a philosophy that has never changed: do the job right the first time, communicate honestly with every customer, and never deliver work you wouldn't be proud to put your name on. Those weren't optional standards in Annapolis — they were the baseline. And for Stephen, they were also something deeper — a reflection of a personal conviction to treat every person with fairness, honesty, and respect.

"Starting this business wasn't just a business decision — it was a step of faith. I believed that if I built something on the right foundation — honest work, fair treatment, and genuine care for people — it would stand. Ten years later, I know that was the right foundation."

When Stephen relocated to South Florida, he didn't just bring his tools and his certifications — he brought his convictions. Founding SN Yacht Services was an act of faith: a decision to build a business not on shortcuts or sales tactics, but on the belief that excellence and integrity, practiced consistently, would speak for themselves. He made a commitment from day one to never compromise those convictions for the sake of a faster dollar or an easier path.

That commitment extends beyond the boat. Throughout his ten years in South Florida, Stephen has made it a priority to give back to the community that has supported him — through service, generosity, and a genuine investment in the people and neighborhoods around him. Business success, in Stephen's view, is not measured only by revenue — it's measured by what you give back and who you lift up along the way.

Over a decade later, that faith has been vindicated. Hundreds of vessels serviced, a return-customer rate that speaks louder than any advertisement, and a reputation built entirely on how people are treated — from the first phone call to the final walkthrough. The name SN Yacht Services stands for more than marine electronics. It stands for what happens when a person of conviction builds something they believe in.

The Journey

How SN Yacht Services Was Built

The Beginning — Annapolis, MD
Foundation Built in Annapolis

Stephen Nemeth begins his marine electronics career working on boats in Annapolis, Maryland — immersed in one of America's most demanding boating communities. The three principles that would define his career are established here: excellence in craftsmanship, unwavering customer service, and an absolute refusal to cut corners.

Certifications & Specialization
Credentials Earned, Expertise Deepened

Stephen earns his Garmin Factory Certification, Airmar Installer Certification, NMEA Electronics Installer Certification, and ABYC Marine Electrician Certification — building a credential portfolio that few independent installers in South Florida can match. Specialization in NMEA 2000 network integration and transducer systems sets him apart.

South Florida — SN Yacht Services Founded
A Step of Faith into South Florida

By faith, Stephen establishes SN Yacht Services in Boca Raton, Florida — making a personal commitment to build a business without compromising his convictions: treat every customer fairly, do the work right, and never sacrifice integrity for profit. The business is built on referrals from day one, and the community responds. Word travels fast when the work — and the character behind it — speaks for itself.

Product Brand Launch
SN Yacht Products Born

Drawing on years of hands-on installation experience, Stephen launches SN Yacht Products — his own line of marine push button switches and rocker switch covers manufactured and assembled in South Florida. The same components used in his custom panel builds are now available to boaters nationwide through snyacht.com.

Faithful to the Foundation

Over a decade in business, hundreds of vessels serviced, and a reputation built entirely on the quality of the work and the character of the person behind it. Throughout those ten years, Stephen has remained committed not just to his customers but to his community — giving back consistently and investing in the people around him. The convictions established at the beginning have never been compromised. The faith that started it all continues to sustain it.

Official Certifications

Credentials That Matter

Certifications aren't just badges — they're proof of factory training, technical competency, and a commitment to doing the job to the highest professional standard.

🏆
Garmin Factory Certified Installer
Garmin International

Factory-trained and certified by Garmin to install, configure, and service the complete Garmin marine electronics lineup. Certification ensures full factory warranty coverage on every Garmin product we install. We are an authorized Garmin dealer for South Florida.

🔊
Airmar Certified Installer
Airmar Technology Corporation

Certified by Airmar — the world's leading manufacturer of marine transducers — for proper transducer selection, placement, and installation across all hull types. Airmar certification is critical for sonar, forward-facing sonar, and CHIRP system performance. An improperly installed transducer negates the technology above it.

🔗
NMEA Certified Electronics Installer
National Marine Electronics Association

NMEA certification validates expertise in marine electronics installation and NMEA 2000 network design. NMEA 2000 is the communication backbone of every modern integrated helm — certifying that every device on your vessel communicates reliably and correctly.

⚡
ABYC Certified Marine Electrician
American Boat & Yacht Council

ABYC certification is the marine industry's highest standard for electrical work safety. Every wire run, connection, and panel installation we complete adheres to ABYC standards — protecting your vessel, your passengers, and your investment. Improper marine electrical work is the leading cause of boat fires.
