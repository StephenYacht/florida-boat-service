Common Questions

Miami Marine Electronics FAQ

Do you offer marine electronics installation in Miami?
Yes — we provide full mobile marine electronics installation throughout Miami and all of Miami-Dade County. We come to your marina, boatyard, or home dock throughout Biscayne Bay, Miami Beach, Coconut Grove, and surrounding areas.
Are you a certified Garmin installer serving Miami?
Yes. We hold Garmin Factory Certified Installer status — making us an authorized Garmin dealer and installer serving Miami and Miami-Dade County. Every Garmin product we install is covered by full factory warranty.
Do you build custom boat dash panels for Miami boats?
Yes — custom CNC dash and switch panels are our signature service. We build them at our Boca Raton fabrication shop and install them dockside throughout Miami. Cast acrylic, stainless steel, laser-engraved labels, backlighting — built to your exact specifications.
Do you install Starlink on boats in Miami?
Yes — Starlink Maritime is extremely popular among Miami boaters, especially those heading to the Bahamas, the Keys, or running long offshore passages from Government Cut or Haulover Inlet. We handle hardware sourcing, waterproof antenna mounting, power integration, and onboard WiFi setup. Most installs are completed in a single day.
How much does marine electronics installation cost in Miami?
Pricing varies based on the specific equipment and scope of work. We offer free estimates with no obligation — call (754) 333-0479 or email info@snyacht.com and we'll discuss your project and provide a detailed quote.
Which Miami marinas do you service?
We service all marinas throughout Miami and Miami-Dade County including Dinner Key Marina, Miami Beach Marina, Bayfront Park, Coconut Grove marinas, and all Biscayne Bay dockages. We come to you.
