Common Questions

Before You Call

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Don't see yours? Call or email — we're happy to talk through any project.

Are estimates really free?
Yes — 100% free, no strings attached. We'll discuss your project, recommend the right approach, and give you a detailed quote with no obligation to proceed. We believe you should know exactly what something costs before you commit.
How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?
Same business day — Monday through Friday, 8am to 5pm EST. If you contact us in the evening or over the weekend, you'll hear back first thing the following business day. Phone calls during business hours receive the fastest response.
Do you do mobile marine electronics installation in South Florida?
Yes — this is our primary way of working. We come to your vessel at your marina, boatyard, or home dock throughout Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. You don't need to move your boat anywhere. We bring everything to you.
How much does a custom boat dash panel cost in South Florida?
Panel pricing varies based on size, material, switch count, backlighting, and complexity. Simple panels start in the hundreds; full CNC-machined cast acrylic panels with backlit switches, laser engraving, and hidden hardware typically range from several hundred to a few thousand dollars depending on scope. Contact us for a free, detailed quote specific to your vessel.
Do you supply the equipment or do I need to purchase it separately?
We supply everything. As authorized dealers for Garmin, Simrad, Raymarine, JL Audio, Fusion, and many other brands, we source, supply, and install all equipment. One point of contact handles the entire project — from product selection through final installation and walkthrough.
How far in advance do I need to book?
It depends on the time of year and project scope. Simpler jobs — a single electronics install or audio upgrade — can often be scheduled within a week or two. Larger full-helm builds may require more lead time, especially during peak boating season. Reach out as early as possible to secure your preferred timeframe.
Do you install Starlink on boats in South Florida?
Yes — Starlink Maritime installations are one of our most requested services. We handle everything from hardware sourcing to waterproof antenna mounting, power integration, and onboard WiFi configuration. Most Starlink installs are completed in a single day.
What certifications does SN Yacht Services hold?
We hold Garmin Factory Certification, Airmar Certified Installer status, NMEA Electronics Installer Certification, and ABYC Marine Electrician Certification. We are also authorized dealers for Garmin, Simrad, Raymarine, JL Audio, and Fusion. These credentials protect your warranty and ensure every installation is performed to the highest professional standard.
