How It's Built

The Fabrication Process

From first measurement to final installation — every step is handled in-house with zero shortcuts.

01
📐
Vessel Measurement & Design

We take precise measurements of your existing helm, console, or dash opening. Every panel is engineered from scratch — no generic templates, no guesswork. Your vessel's exact dimensions drive the design.

02
⚙️
CNC Machining

Panels are precision-cut on CNC machines from premium cast acrylic stock. Cutouts for switches, breakers, gauges, and displays are machined to exact tolerances — no hand-routing, no variation.

03
✨
Laser Engraving & Finishing

Switch labels, graphics, and custom text are laser-engraved for permanent, UV-resistant legibility. Edges are hand-polished to a mirror finish. Stainless steel hardware and hidden mounting fasteners are fitted.

04
🔌
Wiring & Installation

Full wiring harness assembly, LED backlighting integration, and professional installation at our shop or dockside. Every connection is clean, labeled, and built to ABYC standards — no loose ends, ever.

What We Build With

Premium Materials.
Factory-Exceeding Results.

🔷
Cast Acrylic Panels

Higher molecular weight than extruded acrylic — superior UV resistance, better machining characteristics, and a sharper finished edge. Cast acrylic is the professional standard for marine panel fabrication.

🔩
316 Stainless Steel Hardware

Marine-grade 316 stainless throughout. Corrosion-resistant fasteners and mounting hardware that won't pit, rust, or degrade in the harsh saltwater environment.

🔦
LED Backlighting Systems

Clean, even backlit illumination for night-time helm operation. We integrate LED systems directly into the panel build for a factory-integrated appearance — not an afterthought add-on.

🔘
SN Yacht Products® Switches

Our own line of IP67-rated, stainless-housed 19mm push button switches — the same ones we manufacture and sell on snyacht.com. Waterproof, LED-lit, and built to last a lifetime.

🪛
Hidden Mounting Hardware

Clean installs with no exposed fasteners on the face. Hidden mounting systems give every panel a finished, OEM-quality appearance from every angle.

Common Questions

Custom Panel FAQ

How long does a custom panel take to build?
Turnaround time varies depending on complexity and current workload, but most standard panels are completed within 1–2 weeks from final design approval. We'll give you an accurate timeline during your consultation.
Do you come to my marina or do I bring the boat to you?
Both options are available. We have a fully equipped shop in Boca Raton for in-shop builds, and we also offer mobile service across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. We'll discuss what works best for your project.
Can you match or replace my existing panel exactly?
Yes — we can replicate and improve upon your existing panel layout, or we can redesign it entirely to your preference. We take precise measurements to ensure a perfect fit in your existing opening.
What switch types and configurations do you support?
We work with push button switches (including our own SN Yacht Products® line), rocker switches, circuit breakers, gauges, USB outlets, and custom display cutouts. If it can be panel-mounted, we can build it in.
Is backlighting standard or an add-on?
Backlighting is an available option on all custom panel builds. We integrate LED systems directly during fabrication so the result is seamless and factory-clean — not a retrofit. Just let us know during the design phase.
Do you handle the wiring, or just the panel itself?
We handle everything — from fabrication to full wiring harness assembly and final installation. Our ABYC and Airmar certified team builds and installs the complete system. You don't need a separate electrician or installer.
